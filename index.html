Projects

Some of the minor and major projects that were carried out during the course of master's and bachelor's degree program are presented in this section. Concepts learnt and software used are breifed accordingly.

Numerical model of a climate chamber

A numerical model of a physical climate chamber is developed in commercial CFD software, ANSYS Fluent, which is further validated using experimental data performed retreived from the experiments conducted inside the climate chamber. Signal processing, data handling and parts of post processing were done using MATLAB and Python

Aircraft Conceptual Design using CATIA V5

This project aims on designing (detailed conceptual design) a logistics UAV for transportation of medical goods. The UAV is designed to serve in Juma Island in Mwanza region, a landmass cut off from the mainland, where the world’s second largest freshwater, Lake Victoria obstructs the laying of roads for conventional transportation

Learn more

Medical UAV - Prototyping and Aerodynamic Analysis

A fully functional prototype UAV was developed for the purpose of medical payload transport which could carry payloads upto 4 kgs and fly for more than 100 kms. he UAV was propelled with the help of two wing mounted motors with propellers and a conventional empennage. Detailed aerodynamic analysis was carried out to assess the performance in ANSYS Fluent

Learn more

Simulation of a thermal antenna and District heating network

Two-dimensional (District heating network) and three-dimensional (Thermal antenna) multiphysics simulations gives a great sight into the heat transfer and efficiency aspects in the real world. These simulations were carries out in COMSOL Multiphysics softtware, which uses FEM formulation unlike other CFD software that use FVM
